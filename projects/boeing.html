Boeing Commercial Airplanes Autoflight

Internship · Boeing

Supported development of 777-9 commercial airplane Autoflight functions.

  • Validated 777-9 Overspeed Protection autopilot mode Simulink linear model by comparison to transfer functions extracted from flight software through signal frequency sweeps. Produced and analyzed Bode plots and time history data to assure expected stability margins were met.
  • Verified 777-9 Emergency Descent autopilot mode performance requirements through exhaustive high-fidelity simulation. Designed and analyzed cases with varying weight, center of gravity, and other parameters.
  • Created fault trees for key signals and conducted analysis in CAFTA to identify and mitigate potential failures to support safety certification of the display crew alert system in relation to the mode control panel and autopilot system of 777-9.
