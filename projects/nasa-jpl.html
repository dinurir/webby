NASA JPL Guidance & Control

Internship · NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory

Modeled satellite attitude control for a novel alkane propulsion payload using the JPL Small Satellite Dynamics Testbed (SSDT) in MATLAB and Simulink, applying feedforward and feedback control principles for optimization.

Designed multiuse hardware for the SSDT and dynamometer testbed in SolidWorks, and performed stress analysis and inertia analysis as a function of changing dimensions and materials.
